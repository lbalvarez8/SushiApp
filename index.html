ABOUT

What makes a good sushi? That's what we asked ourselves when we started Los Alamos and we have answered this age old question the most mexican way possible. By making a sushi that combines the tradition and style of Japanese sushi with the spicy culinary extravaganza of Mexican gastronomy we have maybe made the best sushi in the world, in not at least the spiciest and deep fried one. We are proudly originated from Culiacan, Sinaloa, land of good food and nothing else don't google it.
